THE QUIET PHONE A smartphone that refuses to notify you
when you are with real humans.

It simply goes dark and silent
the moment it detects three or more heartbeats
in proximity.

No setting.

It just knows:
"Spirit is happening here.
I will wait outside." Simu ya smartphone inayokataa kukujulisha
unapokuwa na watu halisi.

Inachukua tu mwendo wa giza na kimya
sekunde moja inapotambua mishipa mitano au zaidi
karibu nawe.

Hakuna mpangilio.

Inajua tu:
"Roho hapa inacheza.
Nitasubiri nje."
